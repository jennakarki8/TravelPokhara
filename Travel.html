About Pokhara

Pokhara, nestled in the Annapurna range, is renowned for its beautiful lakes, panoramic mountain views, and vibrant adventure opportunities. It's a peaceful city that serves as the gateway to some of Nepal's most famous trekking routes and is known for its natural beauty and tranquil ambiance.

Top Attractions

Phewa Lake

Phewa Lake is the heart of Pokhara, offering serene boating experiences with stunning reflections of the Annapurna range. The Tal Barahi Temple, located on an island within the lake, adds a spiritual touch to the beautiful landscape.

Sarangkot Viewpoint

For an unforgettable sunrise over the Himalayas, Sarangkot is a must-visit. It’s also a hub for paragliding enthusiasts who come here to soar above the picturesque city and lakes below.

Davis Falls

Davis Falls is a unique waterfall that flows into an underground tunnel. This natural wonder is an iconic spot for visitors seeking a mix of beauty and mystery.

Culture of Pokhara

Pokhara’s cultural diversity is vibrant, with ethnic groups like Gurungs, Magars, and Brahmins residing here. Festivals like Dashain, Tihar, and Maghe Sankranti are celebrated with traditional music, dance, and festive foods. The local cuisine, especially momos and thakali, offer a unique taste of Pokhara’s rich culinary heritage.
